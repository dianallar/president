Meet Diana

My name is Governor Diana Allar, and I'm running for President to ensure that America can remain strong, happy, healthy, and prosperous. As a Senator some time ago, I introduced and passed the AmeriCare Act, which brought health insurance to millions of previously uninsured Americans and made healthcare a lesser worry for the many. As President, I will continue to fight for accessible and affordable healthcare for all.

As the three-term incumbent Governor of Montana, I have made numerous strides to protect the people and turn Montana into a prosperous bastion, perfect for work, recreation, and life. I've outlawed plot zoning, banned gerrymandering and mid-decade redistricting, and implemented policies to support affordable housing. I've banned Non-Compete Agreements and Harmful NDAS, raised the minimum wage, and fought for workers' rights. I've banned manditory arbitration clauses, installed LGBT Housing and Employment protections, and made Montana an all-around better place to live for everyone. Some other policy accomplishments include making it easier for businesses to set up shop in cities like Missoula and Billings, and expanding abortion access to 18 weeks with exceptions.

I have been serving the people in government for decades, all across this great nation. I believe that, right now, America needs a proven leader who can get things done and has gotten things done. That's why I'm running for President. With your support, we can ensure that America remains prosperous, worker-friendly, economically stable, technologically advanced, healthy, happy, strong, and of ample infrastructure (social, cultural, economic, and physical) for years to come.

This fight will be long and hard. We need your support to make it happen. Crawling back from such a disastrous administration will require a tough fight, and we can't do that without your help. A better America is possible. So, let's make it happen. I'm joined in this fight by my lovely husband, my three kids, and my pet dog, Gerald. My lovely campaign manager Empress, too! You should join them in supporting our movement, to ensure a better future for all Americans.
